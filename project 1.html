Top 3 most watched Indian film trailers on internet.

1. Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017)

Baahubali 2: The Conclusion is a 2017 Indian Telugu-language epic action film directed by S. S. Rajamouli, which is both the sequel and prequel to Baahubali: The Beginning. It resolves the famous cliffhanger "Why did Kattappa kill Baahubali?" and details the son's quest for vengeance and retrieval of his rightful throne.

2. Pushpa 2:The Rule(2024)

Pushpa 2: The Rule is a 2024 Indian action drama film and the highly successful sequel to Pushpa: The Rise, starring Allu Arjun as the titular red sandalwood smuggler. The film was a massive box office hit and is currently available for streaming on Netflix.

3. Devara Part 1

The official trailers for Devara: Part 1, starring Jr. NTR, Saif Ali Khan, and Janhvi Kapoor, were released in late 2024 across multiple languages. Directed by Koratala Siva with music by Anirudh Ravichander, the trailers showcase a high-octane action drama set in a coastal world.
